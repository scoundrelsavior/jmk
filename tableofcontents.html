De Salitto

Italian Fashion Style

De Salitto is an Italian brand founded by fashion designer, Icilio De Salitto. He was educated at the London College of Fashion. The famous French couturier, Pierre Cardin, presented De Salitto with his diploma upon graduation. De Salitto has been a designer of children's fashion since 1993. The De Salitto brand has stores around Italy. Natalia and Oleg Pinyagin are the founders of the De Salitto Fashion House and are the owners of the Stefania brand which is named after their little girl.
