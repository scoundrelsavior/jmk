Lilica Ripilica

Brazil South America

Lilica Ripilica is a Brazilian fashion brand specializing in clothing for girls up to ten years old. Marisol S.A. owns the brands Lilica Ripilica, Tigor T. Tigre, Marisol, Mundo Ripilica, Lov.it, Hapier and Pakalolo. Their brands can be found in their chain of 300 stores and over 4,000 multi-brand points of sale across the country. Mundo Ripilica, the adventures of Lilica, the Koala, is an animated series that debuted in 2017 on Discovery Kids and is now available on several platforms and multi-channels around the world. Marisol S.A. produces around 4 million pieces of clothing and over 140 thousand pairs of shoes per year. Marisol S.A. is headquartered in Jaragua do Sul, Brazil, South America.
