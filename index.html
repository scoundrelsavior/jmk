JonBenet Ramsey

Aug 6 1990 ~ Dec 25 1996

JonBenet Patricia Ramsey was born August 6th 1990 at Northside Hospital in Atlanta Georgia to Patricia Paugh Ramsey and John Bennett Ramsey. Her compound first name was derived from her father's name, John Bennett. She died on December 25th, 1996 at the age of six in Boulder Colorado. Her body was returned to Atlanta Georgia. Her funeral was held at Peachtree Presbyterian Church on New Years Eve 1996. She was laid to rest at St James Episcopal Cemetery. Also buried nearby are her mother Patricia Ramsey, her older half sister Beth Ramsey, and her grandparents Donald and Nedra Paugh.

December 29th 2024

I will fight extradition back to America in any country I might be arrested in by U.S. authorities. The likelihood of contamination in such a confined environment was high but that is not my problem. If I am legally required to submit to any type of forensic testing, demanded by law enforcement, I will fight their demands in court.

If I am charged with any crime related to the murder of JonBenet Ramsey, once in an American court, I will plead 'not guilty' to all charges brought against me. At that point, I will be prepared to fight all charges against me with the best attorneys I can acquire.
