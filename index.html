In Loving Memory

Polly Hannah Klaas

3 Jan 1981 - 1 Oct 1993

This beautiful photo of Asters and their resident bee was taken by J L Barnett at his front gate on September 24th 2024 in Polly's very own Petaluma California. When he sent it to me, he wrote, "I took this photo for you." Thank you, Mr Barnett. What you have given, I now submit in loving memory of Polly Hannah Klaas. We love you so much, dearest Polly. We will miss you for always. (Photo by J L Barnett / Photography Designed by John Mark Karr)
